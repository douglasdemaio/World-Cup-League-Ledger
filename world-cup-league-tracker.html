Where the goals
come from

Every World Cup goal, assist and save, credited back to the league the player earns his living in. Toggle between the 2026 tournament (live) and the full 2022 final tally. Tap a league to see exactly who delivered.

Method & sources

Clubs are the player's club at the time of each tournament; goals exclude own goals (2022: 170 player goals + 2 own goals = 172 official). 2022 goal and assist lists from worldfootball.net; 2022 goalkeeper saves from FOX Sports (regulation saves, shootouts excluded). 2026 data compiled from official match reports and updates as the tournament progresses. One judgment call worth knowing: Cristiano Ronaldo's 2022 goal is listed as "unattached" — Manchester United terminated his contract on 22 Nov 2022, two days before he scored.
